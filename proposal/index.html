Project Proposal

DubMatch

Problem/Data Domain

There is currently no way for University of Washington students to find roommates for the upcoming year. Dorm-mates are randomly selected and if students want to live away from student housing, they must know people to get apartments with. Students who are transfers or are new to the university will potentially be paired with roommates they dont get along with or do not share any interests. Providing a website for UW students to find others with similar interests, price-ranges, majors or other factors. There are several existing roommate finding apps currently on the market. These exist for the general public and match people from around a city or region. Currently, many students are forced to find roommates for dorms through social media which filters out those without access to social media. These other apps arent made for students and dont include functionality that would be useful for finding student roommates.

App Description

Users of this application are students who would be looking for apartments/dorms to share.

This app would have a positive impact on minority groups of people by being inclusive and allowing them to express themselves with their race, sexuality, and age on their profiles so they can connect with others.

Users will be able to look for apartments/dorms to share with other students who share the same preferences with them with this app. The first Interactive features we are expecting to provide with this app are filters for users to find other users who share the same preferences as them like price range, age, and sexuality. This app will help solve our chosen problem by making it easier for users to find people who share their same preferences making it easier to match with them then connect with them.
